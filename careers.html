How We Hire

A Process That Respects
Your Time

No 6-round interviews. No ghost forms. We keep it simple and move fast.

01
Apply
Send your resume and a short note on why you're a fit to careers@thinksuite.in. No cover letter novels needed.
02
Screening Call
A 20-minute call with our team. We discuss your background, the role, and whether expectations align on both sides.
03
Task / Interview
A short, paid practical task or a 45-minute technical conversation. We evaluate real skill, not textbook answers.
04
Offer
If it's a fit, we move to an offer within 5 business days. Joining timelines are flexible where possible.
