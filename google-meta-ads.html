AI-Optimised Google & Meta Ads That Maximise ROI and Eliminate Ad Spend Waste

We use artificial intelligence to target the right audience, optimise bids in real time, test creatives automatically, and ensure every rupee of your ad budget delivers measurable business results.

Paid advertising on Google and Meta is the fastest way to acquire customers — but without the right strategy and optimisation, budgets disappear with little to show. ThinkSuite manages AI-optimised ad campaigns that cut waste, improve targeting precision, and consistently lower your cost per acquisition over time.

We combine platform expertise with AI-driven tools that analyse audience signals, creative performance, bidding patterns, and conversion data in real time — making adjustments that human managers simply cannot keep up with manually. The result: campaigns that get smarter, more efficient, and more profitable the longer they run.

Why AI-Managed Ads Outperform Manual Management

AI can process thousands of targeting variables, bid signals, and creative performance data points simultaneously — adjusting campaigns within hours, not days. This continuous intelligence loop means your budget is always being optimised for the lowest cost per conversion, not just set and forgotten.

Frequently Asked Questions

What minimum budget do you recommend for running ads?
We typically recommend a minimum ad spend of ₹15,000–₹30,000/month to gather enough data for AI optimisation to work effectively. Our management fee is charged separately. Budgets can scale up as results improve.
How quickly will my ads start generating leads?
Most campaigns generate their first leads within 48–72 hours of launch. The first 2 weeks are a learning phase where AI optimises targeting and bidding. Results improve significantly from week 3 onwards as the algorithm learns your best-converting audience.
Do you manage both Google and Meta ads, or just one?
We manage both platforms, independently or together depending on your goals. For most businesses, running Google Ads (intent-based) and Meta Ads (discovery-based) together delivers the best full-funnel results.
How do you report on performance?
You receive access to a live performance dashboard plus weekly updates and monthly reports — covering spend, impressions, clicks, leads, cost per lead, and ROAS. Everything is explained in plain language, not just numbers.
